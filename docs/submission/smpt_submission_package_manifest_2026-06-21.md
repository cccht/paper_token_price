## Official Checks Used

- SMPT guide for authors:
  https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/simulation-modelling-practice-and-theory/publish/guide-for-authors
- SMPT scope emphasizes original modelling and simulation work, transparent model
  development, implementation, mathematical issues, and verification/validation.
- The current guide states:
  - Abstract must not exceed 250 words.
  - Keywords must be 1 to 7.
  - Highlights are encouraged, separate editable file, 3 to 5 bullets, each
    no more than 85 characters including spaces.
  - Equations and tables should be editable text.
  - All tables and figures must be cited and numbered in order.
  - Research data should be deposited and cited, or a statement should explain
    why it cannot be shared.
  - Generative AI use in manuscript preparation must be declared when used.
  - Generative AI or AI-assisted tools are not permitted to create or alter
    submitted figures/artwork unless part of the research method.

conceptual Draw.io schematic with an editable `.drawio` source and deterministic
PNG preview generated by `experiments/build_market_schematic_drawio.py`.

Because AI assistance was used in this Codex workflow to draft/check the Figure 1
layout and script, the safest submission route is:

1. Open the `.drawio` file in Draw.io desktop.
2. Manually review every label and arrow against the manuscript.
3. Export the final image from Draw.io desktop as author-approved artwork.
4. If the journal asks about AI-assisted artwork, disclose the workflow honestly.

Do not submit the earlier imagegen PNG drafts as manuscript figures.
